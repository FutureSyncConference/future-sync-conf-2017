track 2: education

about

With digital technologies becoming increasingly prevalent in our world, all forms of education rely upon technology in some way. By discovering innovative teaching methods, how coding can help learners excel across subjects and the role of digital in the classroom you can engage students in interesting ways.

Through the sharing of ideas, intriguing talks and practical workshops, you will find new ways to lead the next generation of learners out into the world.
