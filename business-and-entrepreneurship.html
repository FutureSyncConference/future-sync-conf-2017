track 3: entrepreneurship and business innovation

about

Digital technology has revolutionised the way businesses work in recent years, as well as leveling the playing field for entrepreneurs to thrive. Future Sync offers a wide-ranging track of activities for you to find out why diversity is vital for long-term success, how responding to rapid change can multiply results whist spending time with some of the brightest business minds from around the South West.

Through a day of talks, networking, discussion and workshops, you can discover how to better leverage the latest innovations to increase value and forge successful new partnerships.
